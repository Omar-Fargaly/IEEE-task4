About Our Front-end

We are the IEEE Front-End Track team, dedicated to helping students become skilled front-end developers. Our team provides interactive sessions, hands-on tasks, and mentorship to guide you through learning modern web development.

Interactive Sessions

Weekly lectures to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js.

Hands-on Tasks

Practical assignments and mini-projects to apply what you learn.

Mentorship

Continuous support and guidance from experienced developers.

Our Services

We provide full support to help students grow and improve during the front-end learning journey.

Technical Support

We help you solve coding issues, explain concepts, and guide you step by step.

Code Reviews

We review your tasks and give feedback to make your code cleaner and more professional.

Learning Roadmaps

Clear, organized paths showing exactly what to learn from zero to advanced.

Responsive Website Building

We help students build complete responsive websites using modern front-end technologies and clean, professional layouts.
